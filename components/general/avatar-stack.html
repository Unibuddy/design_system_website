AvatarStack

Renders a list of Avatars stacked one on top of the other, where the first one is visually on top, and the rest are stacked underneath with some offset to reveal a part of the Avatar.

Limit To

By default, the AvatarStack limits its children to 3, but you can change that option with the limitTo prop.

Size

The gap between the avatars is controlled by the size prop.
